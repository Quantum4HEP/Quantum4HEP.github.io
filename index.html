Welcome to Our Website

Quantum4HEP is a small group of researchers dedicated to develop quantum algorithms for the High Energy Physics (HEP) field of study. It started with the collaboration of three PhD students at the University of Bologna, two in physics and one in data science and computation specialized in quantum computing, as a way to bridge the gap between the theoretical advantages of this new technology and their practical applications.
